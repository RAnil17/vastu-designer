Ideal Plot Characteristics

✓ Preferred Features

  • • Square or rectangular shape
  • • North or East facing entrance
  • • Higher elevation in South-West
  • • Clean, fertile soil
  • • Good drainage system

✗ Avoid These

  • • Triangular or irregular shapes
  • • South or West facing entrance
  • • Lower elevation in North-East
  • • Rocky or barren soil
  • • Poor drainage or waterlogging
