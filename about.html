About Vastu Designer

Bridging Ancient Wisdom with Modern Living

Our Story

Founded in 2010, Vastu Designer emerged from a deep understanding that modern living often disconnects us from the natural harmony that our ancestors instinctively knew. Our journey began when our founder, Acharya Rajesh Kumar, witnessed the transformative power of Vastu Shastra in his own life.

After experiencing remarkable improvements in health, relationships, and prosperity after implementing Vastu principles in his home, he dedicated his life to sharing this ancient wisdom with others. What started as a personal transformation has now touched thousands of lives across India.

Today, Vastu Designer stands as a bridge between ancient Vedic wisdom and contemporary lifestyle needs, helping families and businesses create spaces that nurture their dreams and aspirations.

Our Mission & Vision

Mission

To empower individuals and organizations with the knowledge and tools to create harmonious living and working environments that promote health, prosperity, and spiritual well-being.

Vision

To make Vastu Shastra accessible to everyone, helping create a world where every space resonates with positive energy and supports the highest potential of its inhabitants.
