Vastu Fundamentals

What is Vastu Shastra?

Vastu Shastra is an ancient Indian science of architecture and design that harmonizes the five elements of nature with human dwellings. The word "Vastu" means dwelling or building, and "Shastra" means science or knowledge.

This 5,000-year-old science is based on the principle that everything in the universe is interconnected through energy. Vastu helps create spaces that resonate with natural energies, promoting health, prosperity, and spiritual well-being.

The fundamental belief is that when we live in harmony with nature's laws, we experience peace, success, and abundance in all aspects of life.

The Five Elements (Panchabhutas)

1

Earth (Prithvi)

Represents stability, foundation, and material wealth. Associated with the southwest direction.

2

Water (Jal)

Symbolizes flow, purification, and emotional well-being. Associated with the northeast direction.

3

Fire (Agni)

Represents energy, transformation, and power. Associated with the southeast direction.

4

Air (Vayu)

Stands for movement, communication, and mental clarity. Associated with the northwest direction.

5

Space (Akash)

Embodies expansion, consciousness, and spiritual growth. Associated with the center (Brahmasthan).

Room-by-Room Vastu Guidance

Main Entrance

Ideal Direction: North, East, or Northeast

Best Practices:

  • • Door should open inward
  • • Avoid facing south or southwest
  • • Keep entrance clean and well-lit
  • • Avoid placing shoes near entrance
  • • Use auspicious symbols like Om or Swastika

Master Bedroom

Ideal Direction: Southwest or South

Best Practices:

  • • Head should face south or east while sleeping
  • • Avoid sleeping with head towards north
  • • Keep bedroom in southwest corner
  • • Use calming colors like blue or green
  • • Avoid mirrors facing the bed

Kitchen

Ideal Direction: Southeast or Northwest

Best Practices:

  • • Cooking should face east
  • • Keep gas stove in southeast
  • • Water sources in northeast
  • • Avoid kitchen in northeast
  • • Use fire element colors (red, orange)

Living Room

Ideal Direction: North or East

Best Practices:

  • • Place seating facing north or east
  • • Keep center open (Brahmasthan)
  • • Use warm, welcoming colors
  • • Avoid heavy furniture in center
  • • Ensure good ventilation

Prayer Room

Ideal Direction: Northeast or East

Best Practices:

  • • Face east or north while praying
  • • Keep idols in northeast
  • • Use white, yellow, or orange colors
  • • Keep room clean and peaceful
  • • Avoid placing under staircase

Study Room

Ideal Direction: North or East

Best Practices:

  • • Study table should face east or north
  • • Keep books in southwest
  • • Use green or blue colors
  • • Ensure good lighting
  • • Avoid study room in southwest

Common Vastu Remedies

Missing Corners

If your plot or house has missing corners, it can cause various problems.

Remedies:

  • • Plant trees in missing corners
  • • Use mirrors to extend the space
  • • Place heavy objects in missing areas
  • • Use crystal pyramids

Toilet in Northeast

Toilet in northeast can cause financial and health problems.

Remedies:

  • • Keep toilet seat closed
  • • Use blue or black tiles
  • • Place salt in corners
  • • Install exhaust fan

Kitchen in Northeast

Bedroom in Northeast

  • • Keep room well-ventilated

Main Door Problems

Remedies:

  • • Keep entrance clean
